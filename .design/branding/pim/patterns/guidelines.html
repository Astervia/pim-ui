Foundation

The brand in one breath

PIM is a Rust IP-level proximity mesh overlay. The brand sits in the resist-the-backbone camp alongside Briar, Meshtastic, Reticulum — but unlike any of them, it treats legibility as load-bearing infrastructure. The protocol is documented, the crypto is named, the failure modes are listed.

Emotional compass

The quiet confidence of an instrument you can read from first principles — one that keeps transmitting when the backbone goes quiet.

Archetype

Sage (primary) + Outlaw (secondary). The Sage shows its work. The Outlaw edge appears only when the category earns a pointed observation.

Color

Phosphor green world

Monochromatic green family with one contrast anchor (amber). Everything exists in a phosphor-screen palette. No blue. No purple. No gradients.

Voice

Declarative, Legible, Alive

PIM writes in declarations, not hedges. It names crypto primitives. It acknowledges what it doesn't do. The Outlaw edge appears only when the category earns it.

Do / Don't

Writing the brand

The protocol is documented in docs/PROTOCOL.md.
We've tried to document most of the protocol.
Peer session closed: key exchange failed. See docs/SECURITY.md §3.2
There may have been an issue with the peer connection.
TCP today. Wi-Fi Direct when the transport layer catches up.
We support TCP now but plan to support more transports later.
X25519 for key agreement. ChaCha20-Poly1305 for authenticated encryption.
Military-grade end-to-end encryption protects your data.
